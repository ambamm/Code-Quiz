# Code-Quiz


GIVEN I am taking a code quiz

WHEN I click the start button

THEN a timer starts and I am presented 
with a question

WHEN I answer a question

THEN I am presented with another question

WHEN I answer a question incorrectly
THEN time is subtracted from the clock

WHEN all questions are answered or the timer reaches 0

THEN the game is over
WHEN the game is over

THEN I can save my initials and score



<div> to hold the quiz
<button> to submit the quiz
<div> for the timer interval 
<div> to display the results 
     

Come up with a way to show first question, their choices and the 4loops that show questions and choices stops at the click of a button and them moves on. 

Local storage: FInput, they need their initials with a click of submit and retrieve what they put in there and with their score, put in local storage.
